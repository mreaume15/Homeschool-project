HomeLog is a social media platform for your homeschool groups


  • Connect with other parents and students in your area
  • Share support and encourgment with other homeschool familys
  • Make student groups easily and invite your friends to your teaching group
  • Sign up to teach a class for your group, either one time or for the entrie semester
  • Plan your Childs classes, field trips and activity all in one feature
  • Track your childs grades and activitys all in one place

the groups meet three times weekly for classes and once per month for a activitys and field trips classes rotate homes 3x weekly, covering diffrent subjects each week. two days per week are reserved for study or extra curricular activitys.
